⚡ **Solar-Powered EV Charging Station with Battery Energy Storage System**

A MATLAB/Simulink-based Solar EV Charging Station integrating MPPT, Battery Energy Storage System (BESS), Bidirectional DC-DC Converter, and CC-CV EV Charging for efficient renewable energy utilization.

---

📖 Overview

This project presents the design and simulation of a Solar-Powered Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging Station using MATLAB/Simulink.

The system utilizes a Photovoltaic (PV) Array as the primary energy source and implements a Perturb & Observe (P&O) Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) algorithm to extract maximum available solar power.

A Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) is integrated through a Bidirectional DC-DC Converter to store excess solar energy and supply power during low irradiance conditions.

A Buck Converter with Constant Current–Constant Voltage (CC-CV) charging strategy is used for safe and efficient charging of the EV battery.

🔄 Working Principle

1. The PV Array converts solar energy into electrical energy.
2. The MPPT controller continuously extracts maximum power from the PV array.
3. The Boost Converter regulates the DC Bus voltage.
4. The Bidirectional Converter charges or discharges the Station Battery depending on solar power availability.
5. The Buck Converter performs CC-CV charging of the EV Battery.
6. SOC protection ensures safe operation of both batteries.
